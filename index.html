Cabbage rolls

Ingredients Amount
Cabbage rolls 1 kg
Cabbage One big cabbage
Water 3 litres
Rice 75 grams
Onion 1-2 onions
Minced meat 500 grams
Garlic 1-2 garlic cloves
  1. Carefully dig out the cabbage root with a sharp knife. Put the head of cabbage in a large pot to boil in salt water. Separate the top leaves as they begin to soften - cool quickly in cold water so that the cabbage leaves do not soften further.
  2. Boil the rice until semi-soft. Lightly fry the chopped onion in butter. In a large bowl, mix the cooked rice, minced meat and the remaining filling components, season well.
  3. Put a spoonful of meat-rice mixture on each cabbage leaf and fold the sheet into a package.
  4. Place the cabbage rolls in a baking dish, pour hot (cabbage) water or broth into the mold and spread a few pieces of butter on top. Bake in a 200 degree oven for 50-60 minutes.
